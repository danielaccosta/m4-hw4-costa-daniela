Growing Up Adele

Adele was born on May 5, 1988 in Tottenham, London. She grew up with her mom Penny Adkins and her father Mark Evans. Evans left Adele's mom and Adele when she was only 2 years old.When Adele was 4, she started singing and loved it. When she was older she atteneded and graduated from a Preforming Arts School in Croydon.

Family Life and the Impact on Music

After Adele graduated school and dated Simon Konecki in 2011. A year later Adele got pregnant and married the father of her son in 2017 in a private ceramony. After 7 years of marriage her and Simon had divorced and during this time Adele took a step back from music until 2021.

During this break, she found out her dad had cancer and was passing away. Adele healed her relationship with her father shortly before and played him a few of her songs she wrote about him. She told the story of her childhood and her anger from her father in her most recent album.
